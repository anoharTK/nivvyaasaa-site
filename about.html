About Nivvayasa Developers & Builders

Nivvayasa Developers & Builders, established in 2023, is one of the fastest-growing and trusted real estate brands in Karnataka.

We successfully launched our pilot project in Bangalore Rural – a premium 12+ acre converted residential layout in Sondekoppa, Nelamangala. The entire project was sold out in record time.

Currently, we are executing multiple high-value projects across Karnataka, with our flagship ongoing project in SIRA. Bookings are now open and filling fast due to high demand and excellent connectivity.

At Nivvayasa, we focus on transparency, quality development, clear titles, and premium customer experience — ensuring long-term value for every client who becomes part of our journey.

We are committed to delivering luxury-grade layouts, sustainable planning, and future-ready infrastructures that redefine residential living.

With strong market confidence and growing investor trust, Nivvayasa continues to expand rapidly with new projects, strategic land development, and long-term vision for urban lifestyle enhancement.

Our dedicated team ensures every project follows premium standards, government regulations, and top-tier design quality.

Your dream plot, your future investment — Nivvayasa makes it happen with integrity and excellence.
