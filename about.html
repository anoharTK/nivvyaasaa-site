M C Narasimha Murty

M. C. Narasimha Murty is a distinguished public servant who has dedicated his life to the service of the people of Karnataka. Serving the Government of Karnataka as a Tahsildar, he has played a vital role in implementing developmental initiatives and administrative reforms that have positively impacted countless citizens. Beyond his official duties, Mr. Murty is widely recognized for his philanthropic contributions and deep commitment to community welfare. His charitable works span across multiple sectors — from extending financial assistance to temples, schools, and orphanages to supporting individuals and families in need. His selfless service has drawn the attention of various media houses, leading to several television interviews that highlighted his inspiring humanitarian efforts. He was notably featured on Public TV’s program “Belaku,” where his initiative to rescue people from a rare illness in a remote village was commended as an extraordinary act of compassion and leadership. Under his guidance and support, significant developmental work was carried out at the historic Shivaganga Temple near Tumakuru, preserving its cultural and spiritual heritage for future generations. Through his integrity, dedication, and generosity, M. C. Narasimha Murty has earned deep respect across the state — not only as an able administrator but also as a shining example of humanity and service.

Welcome to NIVVYASASAA

Nivvyasaa Developers & Builders was established in 2023 with a vision to create value-driven residential communities across Karnataka. From the beginning, the company has focused on trust, transparency, and quality as its core pillars. Nivvyasaa successfully launched its pilot residential project in Bangalore Rural, Nelamangala, developed across more than 12 acres of converted land in Sondekoppa. The project received an overwhelming response, with every plot sold out in record time. This achievement strengthened the company’s commitment to delivering well-planned, legally clear, and customer-centric layouts. Building on this success, Nivvyasaa is currently running multiple residential projects across the state. Among them, the ongoing project in Sira has gained exceptional demand, with bookings moving rapidly. The company’s growing customer base reflects its consistent delivery of trust and quality. Nivvyasaa aims to provide affordable yet premium living spaces for families seeking secure investment and long-term value. Each project is backed by proper documentation, clear titles, and well-designed infrastructure. With a young and energetic team, Nivvyasaa continues to expand its footprint across Karnataka’s real-estate landscape. The company remains dedicated to turning land into thriving communities while ensuring satisfaction for every customer who becomes part of the Nivvyasaa family..
